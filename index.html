What to Do with Your Existing Paper Wallets

Your paper wallets are safe as long as you keep the private keys secret. Do not lose them! Here's how to access your funds:

1
Download Electrum (free Bitcoin wallet): Download Electrum
Verify the download with GPG signatures.
2
Create a new wallet or use an existing one.
Go to Wallet > Private Keys > Sweep.
3
Enter your private key (starts with 5, K, or L, or WIF format).
Click Sweep. Funds will move to your new wallet address.
